# Privacy Policy for Instagram Fast Forward

**Last updated:** March 15, 2026

## Overview

Instagram Fast Forward is a Chrome extension that allows users to fast forward Instagram videos by holding the spacebar.

## Data Collection

This extension **does not collect, store, or transmit any user data**. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No browsing history is tracked
- No cookies are created or read
- No data is sent to any external servers
- No analytics or telemetry is used

## How It Works

The extension runs a content script exclusively on instagram.com that:

1. Listens for spacebar key events
2. Adjusts the playback speed of the currently visible video element

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

## Permissions

The extension only requires access to `www.instagram.com` to modify video playback speed. It requests no other permissions.
